GlassFish AppServer Chat room

The GlassFish community uses #glassfish at freenode.net (use irc://irc.freenode.net).

To chat you first need an IRC client. Then, the first time you will need to register using

/msg NickServ REGISTER ''yourPassword''

Note that yourPassword is not secured. In later visits you will be using that password to identify yourself with the
command

/msg NickServ IDENTIFY ''yourPassword''

To join the GlassFish channel, then

/join #glassfish

A log of converstation in the GlassFish channel is kept at http://www.uwyn.com/drone/log/bevinbot/glassfish.

--------------------

Installing Clients

There are many clients available free and xchat seems be the most popular: http://www.xchat.org/

Check out free Win32 XChat clients: http://silenceisdefeat.org/~b0at/xchat/win32/.

There is also a very extensive list of clients (with reviews) at http://www.ircreviews.org/clients/.

Or if you already use a recent version of the popular gaim instant messaging client, you can probably configure
it to connect to irc. Some instructions are available at http://www.kplug.org/ircchatsetup/gaim-irc/index.html. Just be sure to join #glassfish instead of (or in addition to) the channel mentioned in these instructions.

If you are behind a firewall, you will need to configure your client to work with an HTTP or SOCKS proxy.
