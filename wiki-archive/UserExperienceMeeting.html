User Experience Meeting

We are planning to start weekly community meetings with a goal of improving overall user experience for GlassFish. Our current plan is to review the out-of-the-box experience of new GlassFish v2 features. This will be an excellent forum to express thoughts on the proposed features and clarify enhancement requests. Module leads would present their features and discuss usability aspects to gather inputs. Presentations will be sent out to the users@glassfish.java.net alias before the meeting.

All OnePagers are here. Presenters, please capture the Minutes in wiki pages of the form UE_foobar_Minutes.
