Do I need to log in? How do I register?

To edit pages and contribute to GlassFish Wiki you must first log in. If all you want to do is browse around and read content, you do not need to log in.

To log in

Click Log in at the top of a GlassFish wiki page and authenticate on the login page that displays.
If you don't have an account, you'll need to register as a new user as described below before you can log in.

To register as a new user

  1. Click Log in at the top of a GlassFish wiki page, then click the Register New User tab or the Register with GFWiki Now! link.
  2. Provide the requested information on the registration page that displays and click Create User Profile.
  3. After registering, contact [wiki@glassfish.java.net] and request that write access be granted to your account. This enables you to add and edit wiki pages. This unfortunate inconvenience became necessary due to wiki spam and vandalism. Important: If you do not request write access as described above you will not be able to add or edit wiki pages. This is also discussed in Why can't I edit a GlassFish Wiki page?
