FAQ Translation Status

This page lists the User and Developer FAQ pages on the GlassFish wiki that need to be translated from English into other languages. The information is organized in tables, by language. Translations into other languages are welcomed and encouraged. Just add a corresponding table to the list, keeping the list in alphabetical order for ease of use.

Feel free to add other FAQs to the table! What's listed now is just a start and an example. Translate whichever FAQs you wish, then record them in the table (or in some way list and point to the translation work that's been done). Please also send an e-mail to both the [doc team] and the [G11n team], describing what you're working on or what you've finished. That allows other community members working on translation to get in touch with you and also makes it possible to appropriately acknowledge and recognize your contribution.

Before you begin, please see Translating GlassFish User and Developer FAQs. Questions or suggestions about any of this? Please contact both the [doc team] and the [G11n team]. And thanks in advance for your contribution.
