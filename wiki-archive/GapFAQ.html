  • I'm new to GlassFish but eager to contribute, where do I start?
    You'll probably find what you're looking for on the http://glassfish.org project homepage. Make sure you also check out the FAQ.
  • How do I register for the Program?
    See the Gap Rules, Section 3 for details.
  • What kinds of contributions can I submit?
    You can contribute code, documentation, blue prints, courseware, bug reports, portal localizations, user group promotions and other types. See the Gap Rules, Section 4 for details.
  • Can a team register for the contest?
    Yes. See the Gap Rules for details.
  • Can I submit more than one contest entry?

  • How do I contribute to GlassFish?
    See the Gap Rules for details.
  • What are the contest Judging Criteria and Scoring?
    See the Gap Rules, Section 5 for details.
  • When does the contest start and end?
    It starts on January 29th, 2008 and ends on June 30th, 2008.
  • When will the winners be notified?
    August 15th, 2008
  • Can one submit test cases as a contribution?
    Yes
  • Where do I send my entry?
    Submissions are to be sent to Gap-Submissions at GF (Archive, [mailto])
