GlassFish V2 Admin Team Meeting Minutes

24jan2007

To listen to a digital recording of this call, dial 877.471.6587 or 402.970.2667 and enter program ID 1103447182009.This program will be available from 01/24/07 at 12:00 AM CT to 02/23/07 at 11:59 PM CT.

Present:
Paul, Judy, Sreeni, Nga, Sankar, Byron, Jane, Satish, Kedar

Discussion Summary:

  • For the review of man pages the consensus was that the documentation team will check for formatting errors and QA will check for the accuracy of technical content. The scope includes all the man pages not just the changed ones.
  • Paul was OK for QA to file bugs (instead of sending email) for documemtation issues if that was the practise.
  • Sankar mentioned that about 28 bugs were filed and some of them are related to system properties and start/stop commands. Sankar confirmed that if it is a regression bug then the synopsis will contain the same. Kedar confirmed that these issues are being worked on.
  • Regarding priority of bugs and how it is interpreted for the purpose of resolution, Kedar asked the team to refer to recent Bill Shannon's email on this topic.
  • Satish mentioned that he is working on compiling a list of requirements based on recent interaction with Bluecross and Blueshield.

thanks
sreeni
