Load Balancer Admin Meeting

We are planning to meet weekly (as and when needed) community meetings with a goal of improving Load Balancer Administration features in GlassFish. Our current plan is to review the existing features for ease of use, discuss the CLI/GUI issues. Documentation person will dial into this meeting, so any feedback or suggestions to improve documentation will be provided to LB Admin doc team. This will be an excellent forum to express thoughts on the proposed features and clarify enhancement requests.
