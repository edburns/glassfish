GlassFish v3 FCS Launch

The GlassFish journey started at JavaOne 2005. Since then we had multiple releases of GlassFish v1 and of GlassFish v2 and then two partial releases of GlassFish v3: Prelude (Nov 6th, 2008) and Preview (June 9th, 2009). On December 10th, 2009 we take the next step with the first full release of GlassFish v3, scheduled .

To celebrate the launch, we are hosting multiple events:

  • Dec 9th - Pre-Launch
    • Sao Paolo Sun Tech Day.
  • Dec 10th - Official Launch
    • Main pages: Product, JavaEE 6, Downloads
    • BlogFest - Please join us online with Blogs (Umbrella Post) and Tweets (#glassfish and @glassfish).
  • Dec 15th - Free Virtual Conference
    • Full day, 8am-5pm (PT) see announcement, agenda, registration
