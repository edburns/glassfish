What is SailFin v2 ?

SailFin v2 is the next release of SailFin currently scheduled for release in October of 2009. This is again based on GlassFish v 2.x ( i.e. v2.1 +) and JSR289. To know more about what is in SailFin v2, check out this blog:

Overview

SailFin v1 is the first release of SailFin , compliant with JSR289. SailFin v2 intends to build upon SailFin v1 with additional value added features around clustering and high availability and for better performance and scalability.
