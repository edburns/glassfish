Getting to the Sun Office

By Metro

Sun Office is right at Metro line C station named Chodov. When you get to the Chodov take the stairs to the lobby and get off by the first stairs on the left hand side, go straight along the bus station. You will see the business office park ahead of you. Once you get to the business center, Sun's entrance is the second one on the right hand side. Ask the public receptionist to let you in to the lift, the Sun's reception is on the 4th floor.

By Car

Sun has a few visitor parking spots in the underground garage. Here are the GPS coordinates of the garage 50°01′45.48″N 14°29′38.76″E.
Once you get to the garage entrance, the security should be able to help you finding the spot. From the garage, take an elevator to the ground floor and ask the public receptionist to take you to the 4th floor - Sun's reception desk.
