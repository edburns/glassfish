GlassFish Community Acceptance Testing Awards Program (FishCAT Awards Program)

This page contains a description of the FishCAT Awards Program.

Summary

The FishCAT Awards Program ("Contest") is designed to encourage GlassFish community to participate in GlassFish V3 Beta testing to improve GlassFish quality. Prizes will be awarded to top 3 participants who file most valid bugs to earn the most FishCAT points in accordance with the Official Rules.

Here are the steps to participate in the contests:

  • To enter, you must be a member of the GlassFish Quality Community. If you are not, Please Join
  • To enter, you must apply for the FishCAT program by submit the FishCAT application form to quality@glassfish.java.net [Mailto]. All Entries must be received by 11:59 P.M. (PST) on August 4, 2008
  • Follow the FishCAT Awards Program Official Rules
  • Prizes will be awarded to top 3 participants who earn the most FishCAT points in accordance with the Official Rules. Will announce the winers in end of October 2008.

How to earn FishCAT points

FishCAT members will collect CAT points for their contribution:

Activity type CAT points
RFE 5
Bug 5
Review entire doc 10

We will reward 3 best members based on CAT points with iPod Touch (8GB).
