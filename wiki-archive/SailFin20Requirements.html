SailFin 2.0 Requirements

Requirements are being gathered from existing Requests for Enhancements (RFEs), Customer feedback and Community participation.

To provide comments on requirements or to add a requirement, please use the comment link at the bottom of each page or start a thread in the SailFin Users alias (preferable). When describing a requirement, please provide detail and justification, and link to as much relevant content you can find online.
