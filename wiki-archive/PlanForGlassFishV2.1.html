GlassFish v2.1 Release

Scope of Release (Goals)

GlassFish v2.1 is a tightly constrained release to accommodate the SailFin (main page) offering and high priority bug fixes. Module Leads will be evaluating existing and incoming issues to see if they should be considered for integration into the v2.1 release.

Announcements

Jan 23 2009: GlassFish v2.1 is released!!!

Nov 24 2008: GlassFish v2.1 FCS moves to Jan 21st 2009
SailFin (the driver for v2.1) has moved from Dec 15th FCS to January 21st. GlassFish has to move to be in lock-step with the SailFin release. Consequently GlassFish v2.1 has moved to Jan 21st 2009.

Aug 15, 2008 : GlassFish v2.1 Schedule (draft)

The schedule (draft) of GlassFish v2.1 is out. The SCF is Oct 1st, HCF is Nov 5th and the FCS release is December 15th.

This is a work in progress (as it depends on SailFin release) and may change. We should have a finalized schedule in the next week or so.
