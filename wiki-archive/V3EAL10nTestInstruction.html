Welcome to the GlassFish Localization (L10n) testing Community for Project GlassFish v3 EA!

Introduction

This is designed to have community contributors involved in the quality assurance of the localized GlassFish v3 EA as we value highly the feedback from the community members. We welcome everyone who is interested in participating in the L10n testing program and will work closely with all to make it a success.

GlassFish v3 EA is shceduled to be released in 2009. It will support these languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese and Korean. There have more languages if community members contribute new language translations.

How to join and contribute

Please check GlassFish Localization (L10n) testing page for overall information. Here we only provide GlassFish v3 EA specific information.
