GlassFish Quality Community ThinkGeek Certificates Awards Program

This page contains a description of the the awards program.

Program Descriptions

The GlassFish Quality Community ThinkGeek Certificates Awards Program is designed to encourage and recognize Sun employees and none Sun employees contribution to GlassFish Quality Community. It is simply a way to say thank you to the community members after they have done contributions to GlassFish Quality Community. It is one time offer of $100 ThinkGeek Certificates which is in US dolor and can only be used in US. The person gets this awards will receive an email with a certificate code and can use the certificate to shop in www.thinkgeek.com. The certificate codes are good until Dec. 13, 2008. Please read Terms and Conditions to comply the program rule.

Thanks for your contribution to GlassFish Quality Community !
