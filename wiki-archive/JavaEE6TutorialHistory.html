History of the Java EE 6 Tutorial

May 27, 2009 Early Access Release

  • Accompanies the GlassFish v3 Preview release and works with the NetBeans 6.7 Release Candidate IDE.
  • First time the tutorial has been split into two volumes. Volume I contains basic content and Volume II contains advanced topics.
  • The Early Access Release of the tutorial contains only content from Volume I, Basic Concepts. A great deal of the information in the tutorial is still being worked on and many examples are yet to be added.
  • Users are encouraged to contribute to the examples, which are being developed as an open source project at https://javaeetutorial.java.net/.
  • During the Summer of 2009 we will issue updates to the content and the examples. These updates can be obtained through the GlassFish v3 Update Center.
