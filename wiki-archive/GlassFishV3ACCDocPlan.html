Audience

The ACC is used mainly by developers of clients that access EJB components. Administrators may deploy clients developed by others.

Summary of the Feature From a User's Perspective

The new ACC features add a few small user tasks and enhancements to existing user tasks.

Availability

The ACC was not available in Prelude and will probably not be available in the nucleus and web distributions.
