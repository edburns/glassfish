Moscone Center (Unconference space, Hall A)
Free Event

Unconference (from Wikipedia): "An unconference is a conference where the content of the sessions is created and managed by the participants (generally day-by-day during the course of the event) rather than by one or more organizers in advance of the event"

See also the definition of OpenSpace: "Attendees are asked to generate the meeting agenda as well as participate by leading small group break-out sessions during the meeting time. There is usually a facilitator, but no official meeting leader who demands compliance."
