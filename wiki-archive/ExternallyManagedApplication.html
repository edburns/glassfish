Thats almost everything. The only other reason is that if you have servicemix deployed on top of

Basically , as per JBI spec, only the deployment of service unit between the service engine and JBI
is defined. How a service assembly (which contain many SUs to different engines)
gets deployed is internal to the JBI runtime. That integration can be done in a proprietory way
with OpenESB, but not with other JBI implementations.
