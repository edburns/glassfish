Introduction

Build is an important integral of 3.1 project. Build involves building GlassFish 3.1 workspace, packaging, creating distributions and publishing Maven artifacts and distributions to download.java.net.

For 3.1 milestone, the plan is to continue with the Hudson build jobs that runs continuously, nightly and weekly. The weekly job promotes the distributions. Both nightly and weekly bundles include IPS packaging and are published to http://download.java.net/glassfish/3.1.
