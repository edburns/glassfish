Localizing GlassFish v3 EA

Thank you for being interested in localizing GlassFish v3. Before you begin, please notify g11 AT glassfish.java.net so that we all know who is working on which locales. For the entire program, please refer to v3 EA localization program page.

Steps to Contribute Translations

0. If the language you would like to provide translation is not listed below, send email to g11 AT glassfish.java.net and tell us which language you would like to add. We will notify after the language is added. Normally it should take up to 2 days. When you received notification proceed to step 1.

Currently available locales de es fr ja ko zh_CN zh_TW

1. Go to CTI (https://cti.sunvirtuallab.com/community/) and click "Register or log in" at upper right corner. Then click "Register as a new CTI user". Provide necessary information and register if you agree to Terms of Use.

2. After you have your account created, send email to <strike>cti AT sun.com</strike> g11n AT glassfish.java.net requesting write access to GlassFish v3 project. Otherwise you cannot edit translations. If you had any difficulty, report to g11n AT glassfish.java.net so we can support.

3. Click "GlassFish v3 EA" under Projects, you will find target languages listed. Click the one you are interested in then you will see list of files available to localize and their status. Select and click one of them then you are ready to input translations. Remember to save your work when you are done.
Please also refer to a CTI demo movie at CTI help page.

4. Basically translations done in CTI are integrated into l10n bundle every Thursday (Pacific Time). Notification will be sent out to g11n AT glassfish.java.net when new build is available.

Using Localized v3 EA.

For where to download and how to install localization, please refer to install instruction.

Any question/feedback, please send them to G11n AT glassfish.java.net.
