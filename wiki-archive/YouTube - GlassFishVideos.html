YouTube - GlassFishVideos

The official channel is GlassFishVideos. Unlike with playlists, videos are uploaded into the GlassFishVideos only by invitation - we expect to refine the process as we move along.

You will need the password to the account.

  1. You should have the MP4 that you want to upload
  2. Log into GlassFishVideos using the id "glassfishvideos@gmail.com"
  3. Do not link to gmail account!
  4. Click on upload
  5. Upload the MP4
  6. Adjust the title, summary and tags
  7. add a tag for yourself (see below) so it is easier to track all your videos
  8. add the geographic locations for where you live (I get them from wikipedia)
