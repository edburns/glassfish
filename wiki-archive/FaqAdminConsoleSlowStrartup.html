The startup time for the Admin Console is very (VERY!) long. Why?

If this is GlassFish v3 then probably the Admin Console is accessing (or at least trying to) the Update Center looking for available updates. Depending on your Internet connection it may take as much as 5 minutes.
You may try to disable the automatic startup update check, or remove the Update Center plug-in altogether. This FAQ describes how.
